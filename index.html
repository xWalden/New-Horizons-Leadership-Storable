The Journey
Your leadership development path
A multi-month experience blending live workshops, on-demand learning, and assessments — designed to build real capability, not just check a box.
Stage 1 — Prep & Diagnostic
180 Leader Skills Assessment
📋 Self + Manager Assessment ⏱ Month 1
Kick things off with a FranklinCovey Leader Skills Assessment. You'll complete a self-assessment while your manager provides their perspective — giving us a clear baseline to tailor the experience to your needs. Target launch: early May 2026.*
Stage 2 — Core Content
The 6 Critical Practices for Leading a Team
💻 3 Live Virtual Sessions ⏱ 2 Hours Each
The foundational workshop. Learn how to get work done with and through others — covering mindset shifts, delegation, one-on-ones, feedback, and navigating change as a new leader. Reinforced with on-demand resources and AI coaching tools. Target start: late May / early June 2026.*
Stage 2 — Core Content
Navigating Difficult Conversations
💻 Live Virtual Workshop ⏱ 2 Hours
A few months in, tackle the conversations that matter most. Learn to de-stress challenging interactions, give direct feedback without damaging trust, and get better results from tough situations. Reinforced with group coaching.
Stage 2 — Core Content
Coaching
📱 On-Demand Course ⏱ Self-Paced
Adopt a coaching mindset and learn to give impactful feedback that develops your people. This on-demand course lets you learn at your own pace and apply concepts in real time with your team.
Stage 3 — Next Level Learning
360 Assessment
📊 Full Circle Feedback ⏱ Program Completion
Close the loop with a comprehensive 360 assessment — including feedback from your direct reports, peers, and manager. See how far you've come and identify the next horizon in your leadership growth.

Questions
Frequently asked
Everything you need to know about the program.
New Horizons is designed for first-time people leaders — anyone who was promoted from an individual contributor role into a people management position (Manager, Team Lead, or Director) during the 2026 promo cycle.
The program spans several months, starting with the 180 assessment, moving through the core workshops (6 Critical Practices, Navigating Difficult Conversations, and Coaching), and culminating in a 360 assessment. The pacing is intentional — giving you time to learn, apply, and reinforce between sessions.
The live sessions total about 8 hours (three 2-hour sessions for 6 Critical Practices plus a 2-hour Difficult Conversations workshop). The Coaching course is self-paced and on-demand. Assessments take roughly 15–20 minutes each.
Your manager is a key partner in this journey. They'll complete the initial 180 manager assessment to help baseline your development, and they'll be encouraged to reinforce and support what you're learning throughout the program. At the end, they'll participate in your 360 feedback as well.
FranklinCovey is a global leadership development company trusted by thousands of organizations worldwide. Their research-backed frameworks and content — including The 6 Critical Practices, Navigating Difficult Conversations, and Coaching — form the core of our program and are proven to build effective frontline leaders.
